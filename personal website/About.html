About me

Welcome to my personal corner of the internet! I'm Emmanuel Yogo Agiso, a 22-year-old Kenyan passionate about Computer Technology.

Background

I was born and raised in Kisumu, where I developed a deep appreciation for nature and environment due to the presence of Lake Victoria. My journey into IT started when I just joined high school and studied computer studies as a subject and from that moment, I knew my future was computers.

Academic pursiuts

Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Zetech University. My academic journey has been enriched by numerous experiences, I have been able to share key moments with serious companies like Huawei, Safaricom and Cisco networking company. I have also managed to create an e-commerce website during my time in school. I am particularly interested in Graphic design and strive to contribute to the field of web development and Cyber Security.

Interests and Hobbies

Outside of academia, I find joy in playing football and watching documentaries. Whether it's playing football or watching, I believe in maintaining a balance between my academic and personal life. I'm always up for research.

Professional Goals

My ultimate goal is to become a fulltime Graphic Designer and a Cyber Security Analyst. I aspire to make a positive impact in the field of computer technology, leveraging my skills in Cyber sec and Graphic Design
