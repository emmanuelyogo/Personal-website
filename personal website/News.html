Life Story

Welcome to the chapters of my life! Here's a glimpse into the journey that has brought me to where I am today.

Early Years

I was born on 04/08/2002 in Kisumu, where the seeds of curiosity were planted. Growing up, I was fascinated by computers and developed a love for Technology.

Educational Milestones

My educational journey began at Joel Omino Primary School, where I discovered a passion for Mathematics and computer studies. High school brought new challenges and opportunities, leading me to become one the best performing students in my school.

Transition to University

Embarking on my university journey at Zetech University marked a pivotal moment. Here, I delved into Information Technology, met inspiring individuals, and engaged in Wen development and Graphic design

Challenges and Growth

Life isn't without its challenges. Lack of school fees and daily bread tested my resilience and became a turning point in my personal development. Overcoming this hurdle taught me to always become resilience and remain focused in life.

Passion and Pursuits

Fueling my journey is a deep passion for Information Technology. Whether it's playing football or research, these pursuits have provided a sense of purpose and fulfillment.

Looking Forward

As I stand at this juncture, I am excited about Graphic Design and Cyber security. I envision to be one of the best engineers out there, where I continue to delve in Cyber security and Graphic Design.

Join Me on This Adventure

Life is an ever-unfolding story, and I invite you to be part of my narrative. Together, let's embrace the adventures that await and celebrate the lessons learned along the way.
